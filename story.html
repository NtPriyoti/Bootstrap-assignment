WHO ARE WE?

The Burger Company is a 100% Bootstrapped, modern Casual Dining chain of restaurants offering unique tasting, chef curated burgers in a peppy & youthful ambience. Each of our burger is prepared 100% Fresh, 100% Made to Order with In-House Sauce blends and each of our burger tastes unique. Burgers are accompanied by tempting sides and a wide range of Beverages to choose from. Our engaging peppy, youthful, new-age store ambience along with engagement zone with Play Stations, Table Games, Foosball ensures to make TBC a part of your happy moments. The Burger Company was started in 2018 in Gurgaon and started franchising in the end of 2020. Within a short span of 9 months we have grown to a presence of 25 running outlets in 15 cities with a pipeline of 25 more stores scheduled to be opened in 2021. By the end of 2022, we have planned to have 125+ operational outlets PAN India and by 2023 we aim to become Largest Local Burger Brand in Dhaka.
